AEGIS CORP — CORPORATE SALES & DISCOUNT POLICY 2026
====================================================

EFFECTIVE DATE: January 1, 2026
APPROVED BY: Sarah Jenkins, VP of Sales

1. DISCOUNT AUTHORITY MATRIX
-----------------------------
- Account Executive (AE): Up to 10% discount
- Senior AE / Team Lead: Up to 15% discount
- Regional Sales Manager: Up to 20% discount
- VP of Sales (Sarah Jenkins): Up to 35% discount (HARD CAP)
- Any discount above 35% requires CEO + CFO joint approval

2. DEAL SIZE REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------
- Minimum deal size: $5,000 ARR
- Deals under $5,000 are auto-rejected unless bundled with enterprise contract
- Enterprise tier: $100,000+ ARR (requires dedicated Sales Engineer)

3. PAYMENT TERMS
-----------------
- Standard: Net 30
- Extended terms (Net 60/90): Requires Regional Manager approval
- Prepaid annual: Eligible for additional 5% discount

4. COMPETITOR DISPLACEMENT RULES
----------------------------------
- When displacing DataSync, Salesforce, or HubSpot: Additional 5% displacement discount allowed
- Must document competitor name, current contract end date, and pain points
- Displacement deals require battlecard completion before proposal

5. MULTI-YEAR CONTRACT RULES
------------------------------
- 2-year contracts: Additional 3% discount allowed
- 3-year contracts: Additional 5% discount allowed
- Multi-year contracts require VP of Sales sign-off regardless of discount level

6. ESCALATION TRIGGERS (AUTO-FLAG)
------------------------------------
The following scenarios automatically escalate to VP of Sales:
- Discount exceeds 20%
- Deal involves competitor mention
- Custom pricing requested
- Multi-year term proposed
- Deal size exceeds $50,000
- Prospect requests non-standard payment terms

7. COMMISSION PROTECTION
--------------------------
- Full commission on deals at or below approved discount tier
- 50% commission reduction if discount exceeds authority without pre-approval
- Zero commission on deals closed without CRM entry
