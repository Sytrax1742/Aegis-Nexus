AEGIS CORP — STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: SALES PIPELINE FLOW
================================================================

VERSION: 2.1 | EFFECTIVE: Q1 2026
OWNER: Sales Operations Team

PHASE 1: LEAD QUALIFICATION (SDR Team)
----------------------------------------
1. All inbound leads scored using BANT framework (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
2. SDR must complete qualification call within 24 hours of lead assignment
3. Qualified leads (score >= 70) routed to AE within 4 hours
4. Unqualified leads parked in nurture sequence

PHASE 2: DISCOVERY & NEEDS ANALYSIS (AE)
-------------------------------------------
1. AE conducts discovery call within 48 hours of assignment
2. Required outputs from discovery:
   - Prospect company name and size
   - Decision maker name and title
   - Budget range confirmed
   - Current solution and pain points
   - Timeline for decision
   - Number of users/seats needed
3. All data entered into Zoho CRM within 2 hours of call completion

PHASE 3: PROPOSAL & PRICING (AE + Sales Engineer)
----------------------------------------------------
1. Technical demo scheduled within 5 business days of discovery
2. Sales Engineer completes technical requirements assessment
3. AE generates proposal using approved templates
4. Pricing must comply with Corporate Discount Policy
5. Proposal sent to prospect within 3 business days of demo

PHASE 4: NEGOTIATION (AE + Management)
-----------------------------------------
1. Counter-offers logged in CRM deal notes
2. Any discount request above AE authority escalated per Discount Matrix
3. Competitor displacement deals require battlecard review
4. Legal review triggered for:
   - Custom contract terms
   - SLA modifications
   - Data residency requirements

PHASE 5: CLOSING (AE)
------------------------
1. Verbal agreement confirmed with prospect
2. Contract generated from approved templates
3. E-signature sent via DocuSign
4. Payment terms configured per policy
5. CRM deal moved to "Closed Won" within 24 hours of signature

PHASE 6: HANDOFF (AE → Customer Success)
--------------------------------------------
1. Internal handoff meeting within 48 hours of close
2. AE provides: deal summary, technical requirements, key contacts
3. Customer Success sends welcome email and schedules kickoff
4. AE cc'd on first 30 days of communications

KPIs & METRICS
--------------
- Average sales cycle: 45 days (target: 35 days)
- Win rate: 28% (target: 35%)
- Pipeline velocity: $2.1M/month (target: $3M/month)
- CRM data completeness: 85% (target: 95%)
